ABOUT THE PROJECT

Project features

Impact

The goal of our project is to create a positive impact on users utilizing the program.

Change

Change and diversifying users approaches to tackling the climate change issue is important to our group. We wanted to create a product that effectively conveyed how individuals can make an impact in their own countries.

Future

Here at the Green Coders Organization, we value the future generation and their fight against the ongoing climate crisis. Our program aims assist individuals in finding out about their countries climate and how they can help prevent rising temperatures.

Sustainability

Sustainability through global change is at the forefront of Green Coders' mission statement. We want to make sure that everyone has access to proper information regarding our planet.

Our Program

This program utilizes machine learning to predict future climate trends to produce an outline for individuals to reduce their emissions.
